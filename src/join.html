How to Join a Club

Getting involved is simple. Follow these steps to register, attend your first meeting, and become an active member.

Step by Step

Your Path to Membership

From browsing to participating — the entire process takes less than a week.

1

Browse Available Clubs

Visit the Club Categories page and explore all clubs under Technology, Sports, Cultural, and Debate. Read each description and identify the clubs that match your interests and availability.

2

Visit the Student Affairs Office

Head to the Student Affairs Office (Block A, Ground Floor) to collect the club membership registration form, or download it from the university student portal. Bring your student ID card.

3

Fill & Submit the Registration Form

Complete the membership form with your name, student ID, department, and preferred clubs. Submit it to the Student Affairs Office or email it to clubs@university.edu.pk along with your student ID scan.

4

Receive Confirmation & Club Contact

Within 3 working days, you will receive a confirmation email with the club representative's contact, meeting schedule, and your member ID number. Save these details.

5

Attend Your First Meeting

Show up to your first scheduled club meeting. Introduce yourself, get to know other members, and understand the club's upcoming activities, goals, and participation expectations.

6

Stay Active & Get Involved

Maintain at least 70% attendance at club meetings. Participate in events and competitions. Members who show consistent dedication may be nominated for club leadership roles.

Guidelines

Participation Guidelines

All members are expected to follow the code of conduct and participation guidelines of their respective clubs.

📋

Attendance & Commitment

Members should attend at least 70% of scheduled meetings per semester. Notify your club representative in advance if you are unable to attend.

🤝

Respectful Conduct

All members must treat fellow students, faculty, and guests with respect. Bullying, discrimination, or harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.

🎯

Active Participation

Volunteer for events, contribute ideas in meetings, and take on roles that challenge you to grow. Passive membership is discouraged.

📢

University Representation

When representing the university in external events, all members must adhere to the university's code of conduct and dress code policies.

FAQs

Common Questions

Can I join more than one club?

Yes — students may join up to two clubs simultaneously. Ensure your schedule allows for the commitment required by both clubs.

Is there a membership fee?

Most clubs are free to join. Some activity-based clubs (e.g., swimming, travel) may charge a small semester fee. Details are provided at registration.

Can freshmen join in their first semester?

Absolutely. First-year students are strongly encouraged to join clubs early. It is one of the best ways to settle into campus life and build your network.

How do I leave a club if I change my mind?

Submit a written notice to your club representative and the Student Affairs Office at least two weeks before you intend to leave. No penalties apply for leaving at the start of a semester.
